Profile

Welcome to my creative corner! I'm Orsolya Kiss, a design enthusiast currently on an exciting journey to master the art of Front End Development at Career Foundry. With a solid foundation of 11 years in various design domains; from crafting eye-catching POP displays to diving into the world of aquatic play, and more recently, creating stunning 3D architectural renderings, I've always been driven by the desire to bring ideas to life. My love for design has naturally evolved into a curiosity for coding. I'm on a mission to merge the best of both worlds, aiming to design and code beautiful websites. Beyond that, my sights are set on the ultimate goal of developing my own app. This exciting transition is fueled by a genuine passion for turning creative visions into interactive, user-friendly experiences.

What sets me apart? Beyond my creative skills, I bring an infectious energy and a positive attitude to any team. I thrive on collaboration and am eager to work with seasoned professionals in the field, soaking up knowledge and insights to fuel my growth. Join me on this exciting journey as I navigate the intersection of design and code, creating not just websites but digital experiences that leave a lasting impression. Let's connect, collaborate, and build something extraordinary together!
